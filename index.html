Project: Brooklyn Nine Nine Quiz

This quiz was my first webapp, developed for Thinkful. It’s a single page quiz that tests your knowledge of the comedy gold that is Brooklyn Nine Nine. It uses simple and clean JQuery functions to create each quiz page, keep the score and current question numbers displayed in the header and give random feedback on the answers submitted by the user.
